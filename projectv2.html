Openness

We can examine a rather interesting case between one's Openness and their diversity amongst artists. Similarly to the case between Conscientiousness and cohesion, one would think there would be a positive correlation. If a listener is more open to new ideas, they would be open to listening to more artists. However, we see that the opposite is true. The more open a listener is, the less diverse their artists' portfolio is. With this path of logic, one could then take a deeper look into the correlations between Openness and cohesion, as well as Conscientiousness and diversity. Perhaps if both have negative correlation, their converse is positive?
